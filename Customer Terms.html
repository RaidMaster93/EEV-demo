Customer Terms & Conditions

Welcome to EEV Transport. By accessing or using our services, you agree to comply with the following terms and conditions designed to ensure a safe, transparent, and convenient experience for all customers.

  • Customers must be 18 years or older to book a ride.
  • Accurate personal details must be provided during booking.

2. Ride Booking

  • All bookings must include correct pickup and drop locations.
  • Customers must be ready at the pickup point at the scheduled time.
  • Incorrect information may lead to delays or cancellation.

3. Payments

  • All fares displayed are inclusive of applicable charges.
  • Payments must be made only through approved methods.
  • Any fraudulent activity may lead to service termination.

4. Cancelation Policy

  • Customers may cancel rides before the driver arrives.

5. Customer Responsibilities

  • Respect the driver and vehicle at all times.

  • Customers are responsible for any intentional damage caused.

6. Safety Guidelines

  • Emergency contact information must be accurate.

7. Prohibited Conduct

  • Misusing the app or booking system.
